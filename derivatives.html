derivatives

the derivative is the slope of the tangent line to a curve at a specific point. It is used to measure change, telling you how fast something is changing at a specific moment. It can also be used to find the minimum and maximum points of a curve.

a derivative can appear in a question like:

$$ \frac{d}{dx}\;x^2 $$

Or:

$$ y=x^2 \quad \text{find:} \quad \frac{dy}{dx} $$
